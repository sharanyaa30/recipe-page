Healthy Chicken Taco

These tacos are so flavorful and delicious and the entire recipe comes together in 30 minutes from start to finish, which is ideal when you’re wanting to make a healthy dinner during those busy weeknights!

If you’re on the lookout for a Healthy Chicken Tacos Recipe that doesn’t skimp on flavor, you’ve landed in the right place. Tacos might be the ultimate comfort food, but with the right ingredients and a few smart swaps, they can also become a nutritious powerhouse perfect for clean eating. In this guide, you’ll learn how to craft delicious tacos that are packed with lean protein, fresh veggies, and vibrant toppings—all while keeping calories in check.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Olive oil, Lime juice
  • Garlic, sea salt, cumin, chilli powder
  • Tortillas
  • Toppings
    • red onion
    • avocado
    • salsa
    • sour cream

Instructions

  1. Make the Marinade: Whisk together olive oil, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, salt, and cumin.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Coat chicken thighs with the marinade and let sit for 15–30 minutes (or overnight for deeper flavor).
  3. Cook the Chicken: Grill or pan-sear over medium-high heat for 10–12 minutes, flipping halfway. Ensure internal temp reaches 170–175°F.
  4. Rest the Chicken: Remove from heat and let rest for 3–5 minutes to retain juices.
  5. Dice the Chicken: Cut into bite-sized cubes for easy taco filling.
  6. Assemble Tacos: Fill warm taco shells with chicken and your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
